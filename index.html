    Department Spotlight

    Welcome to the Department of Information Science and Media Studies, where innovation meets creativity. Our department is dedicated to providing students with a comprehensive education in the fields of information science and media studies.

    Our Mission

    At the Department of Information Science and Media Studies, our mission is to empower students with the knowledge, skills, and creativity needed to excel in the rapidly evolving fields of information science and media. We strive to foster a community of innovative thinkers, researchers, and practitioners who are passionate about harnessing the power of information and media to drive positive change in society.

    Our Vision

    Our vision is to be a leading department in Information Science and Media Studies, recognized for our innovative research, cutting-edge education, and commitment to community engagement. We aim to produce graduates who are not only knowledgeable in their field but also equipped with the skills, creativity, and critical thinking needed to address the complex challenges of the digital age.

    About the Department

    The Department of Information Science and Media Studies is a vibrant and dynamic academic unit that offers a range of programs in information science and media studies. Our department is committed to providing students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for successful careers in the fields of information science and media.

    We offer a range of undergraduate and graduate programs, including a Bachelor of Science in Information Science, a Bachelor of Arts in Media Studies, a Master of Science in Information Science, and a Master of Arts in Media Studies.

    Our faculty are experts in their fields and are dedicated to teaching, research, and mentoring students. They bring a wealth of experience and knowledge to the classroom, and are committed to helping students achieve their academic and professional goals.

    Mission Statement

    Our mission is to provide high-quality education, research, and community engagement in information science and media studies. We aim to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills in our students, enabling them to excel in their chosen careers and make meaningful contributions to society.
